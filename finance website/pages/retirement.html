Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start planning for retirement?

The best time to start planning for retirement is as early as possible. Starting early allows you to benefit from compound interest and gives you more time to build a substantial retirement corpus.

How much should I save for retirement?

The amount you need to save depends on various factors including your current age, expected retirement age, lifestyle, and inflation. Use our retirement calculator to get a personalized estimate.

What are the best retirement investment options?

The best retirement investment options include a mix of NPS, mutual funds, fixed deposits, and government schemes. The ideal mix depends on your risk appetite and investment horizon.
